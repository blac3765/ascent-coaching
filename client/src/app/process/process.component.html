Coaching is based on Clifton StrengthsFinder Survey

How did StrengthsFinder originate?

Donald Clifton discovered that only modest incremental imporvement can be achieved by "working on" your weaknesses
He also found that geometric improvement can be attained by focusing on and developing your strengths
Strength = Talent + Development

Initial Review of Your 34 Talents (5 sessions)

Develop and understanding of your natural talents - Are they raw, or have they been developed into strengths?
Understand your lesser talents - Are they holding you back?

Development of Talents (6-12 sessions)

Learn to develop and leverage your top talents
Learn to manage lesser talents by using your strengths
