Should I introduce coaching to my organization?

As a senior leader in your organization:

Do you want your leaders to ACHIEVE?
Do you believe this will translate to improved performance?

Do you encourage your employees to GROW?
Will this enable them to fill more senior leadership roles in the future?

Does your culture strive to allow your employees to ENJOY the journey?
Can this be a valuable tool to improve retention and engagement?
